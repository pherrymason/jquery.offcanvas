
Basic
=====
<div class="sp-off-canvas-wrap" data-offcanvas>
    <div class="sp-off-canvas-inner-wrap">
        <a class="left-off-canvas-toggle" href="#">Menu</a>

        <!-- Off Canvas Menu -->
        <aside class="left-off-canvas">
            <!-- whatever you want goes here -->
            blablablaba
        </aside>

         <aside class="right-off-canvas">
            <!-- whatever you want goes here -->
            blablablaba
        </aside>

        <!-- Main content goes here -->
        <p>Blablabslabsdadba df</p>
    </div>
</div>


Basic: Move Inner only
======================
Para mover únicamente el contenido y que los aside queden estáticos:
<div class="sp-off-canvas-wrap" data-offcanvas>
        <aside class="left-off-canvas">
            <!-- whatever you want goes here -->
            blablablaba
        </aside>
        <aside class="right-off-canvas">
            <!-- whatever you want goes here -->
            blablablaba
        </aside>
    <div class="sp-off-canvas-inner-wrap">
        <a class="left-off-canvas-toggle" href="#">Menu</a>
        <!-- Main content goes here -->
        <p>Blablabslabsdadba df</p>
    </div>
</div>
